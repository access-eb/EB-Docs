## Step 1 - Go to Google Ads
    In the left navbar go to **Goals > Summary**
        ![Google Adds - 06](../../src/images/google_ads/06.png)
## Step 2
- Click on the "+ Create conversion action" button
    ![Google Adds - 07](../../src/images/google_ads/07.png)
    - or you can click the **" + " create** on the left navbar > conversion action. 
## Step 3
- select the website option
## Step 4
- Enter the domain > hit scan.

:::danger
Ensure that you are **NOT** using the **GOOGLE ANALYTICS** measurement, if you, do your conversion wont trigger.
:::

## Step 5
-  Click the add conventions manually

## Setp 6 
- Follow the instructions provided by google ads > hit done 

## Step 7
- You will be prompted with the **Tag Set Up**. select **"Use Google Tag Manager"**

## Step 8 
- You will receive the **Conversion ID** and **Conversion Label**. Copy these and save them for later use.
