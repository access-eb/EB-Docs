:::

## Option 1: Retrieve the Tracking ID
1. Log into **Google Ads**.
2. Click on the **Tools & Settings** (wrench) icon.
3. Select **Data Manager**.
   ![Google Ads - 01](../../src/images/google_ads/01.png)
4. Under **Google Tag**, click **Manage**.
   ![Google Ads - 02](../../src/images/google_ads/02.png)
5. Click **Google Tag**.
   ![Google Ads - 03](../../src/images/google_ads/03.png)
6. Your **Google Tag details** will now be displayed.
   ![Google Ads - 04](../../src/images/google_ads/04.png)
7. Copy the **Destination ID**.

## Option 2: Retrieve Tracking ID and Connect to CMS
1. Log into **Google Ads**.
2. Click on the **Tools & Settings** (wrench) icon.
3. Select **Data Manager**.
4. Under **Google Tag**, click **Manage**.
5. Click **See Installation Instructions**.
   ![Google Ads - 05](../../src/images/google_ads/05.png)
6. Enter your **URL** and select your **CMS**.
7. Copy your **Tracking ID** and follow the provided steps to connect Google Ads to your website.
